Rides & Events

Sky Thrill Coaster

The Sky Thrill Coaster takes riders on an exhilarating vertical climb followed by a steep drop, providing an adrenaline-pumping experience with sharp turns and thrilling speeds. With stunning views of the park from the top, this ride is perfect for thrill-seekers who love high-speed excitement and heart-racing moments!

Speed

Go 0-70mph in 1.0 seconds and get fired 209 ft high on the fastest launch coaster!

Twilight Swinger

The Twilight Swinger is a classic swing ride that elevates riders into the evening sky as they soar in circles. With beautifully lit canopies and a breathtaking view of the park, this nostalgic ride offers a peaceful yet thrilling experience, perfect for guests of all ages who want to feel the wind in their hair as they spin high above the ground.

Sky Screamer

The Sky Screamer is an extreme thrill ride that swings riders high into the air while rotating at rapid speeds. With feet dangling and adrenaline pumping, this ride offers a pulse-pounding experience as you soar above the park, flipping upside down and feeling the rush of wind from every direction. Perfect for those who crave intense excitement and breathtaking views of the city skyline!
