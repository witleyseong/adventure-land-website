Frequently Asked Questions

1. What is Adventure Land?

Adventure Land is a theme park offering a wide variety of attractions for all ages, providing moments of fun and adventure in a safe and welcoming environment.

2. How can I contact support?

You can reach support by filling this Form. Our team is available to help with any questions or issues you may have.

3. What are the opening hours?

The park is open every day from April to October, 9 AM to 7 PM. We look forward to seeing you for a day full of fun!

4. How can I sign up for an event?

You can sign up for events through our events page, where you'll find all the necessary information and a registration form.

5. Are there any registration fees?

Some events at Adventure Land may have registration fees. All details about costs will be provided on the specific event page.
